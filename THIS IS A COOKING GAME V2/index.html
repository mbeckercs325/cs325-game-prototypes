THIS IS A COOKING GAME

  • High Concept: THIS IS A COOKING GAME is a cooperative cooking game.
  • Theme: None, I wanted to make my own game.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will have to cooperate in order to complete as many recipes as they can.
  • Gameplay: The goal of the game is to work together to create as many burgers as possible. A burger consists of cooked meat, chopped lettuce and tomato, a bun, and a plate. Players have to assemble all of these before they can deliver the order.
    Player 1 Controls - WASD, E to select
    Player 2 Controls - Arrow Keys, ? to select
    To make a burger players must cooperate to place a plate, bun, chopped lettuce and tomato, and cooked meat all on the same spot. If all of these ingredients are on the same spot, then a burger will appear there. To pass ingredients from player to player, ingredients can be placed on the conveyor belt that goes around the kitchen.
    The stoves are located on player 1's side and the cutting boards are on player 2's side. To chop lettuce or tomatoes or to cook meat a player must place on of the ingredients on the correct spot. A border will appear on the ingredient over 3 seconds. After 3 seconds the ingredient is done. It can then be added to a burger.
    To score points player 2 must take the completed burger and place it in the delivery zone on the right side of the screen. Each burger is worth 1 point.
  • Prototype Goal: This game is evaluating a cooperative multiplayer experience.
  • Features: THIS IS A COOKING GAME is a cooperative cooking game where players have to communicate and figure out what the most efficient way to complete orders is. The environmental challenge of having two seperate spaces makes cooperation more difficult.
  • Setting/Premise: THIS IS A COOKING GAME takes place in a very inconvenient kitchen.
  • Story: Two cooks have to cooperate to create as many burgers as they can.
  • Target Audience: Anyone can pick up and learn how to play the game very quickly. Actually cooperating and getting good at the game will take more time.
  • Play Time: A round lasts 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Players will have to talk and figure out what ingredients to prioritize and when to try to pass ingredients around.
